About

Hi! I'm Nick, head (and only) tailor at Nilsson tailoring.

My passion for tailoring was born out of a desperation to find clothes that would fit, and as a stocky man this dream was never fulfilled with clothes off the rack.

I realized that tailoring would be the only way to get the perfect fit, and do I decided to learn, and the rest is history...
